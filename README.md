# HR-Analytics-Dashboard-PowerBI

### Project Overview
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing employee attrition and workforce trends. Covers department, age group, gender, education field, job satisfaction, and income insights to help HR teams identify high-risk segments and support data-driven retention strategies.

### Objectives
- Analyze overall employee attrition
- Identify department-wise and gender-wise attrition
- Understand attrition by age group and education field
- Track job satisfaction ratings
- Provide actionable insights for HR decision-making

### Key Metrics
- Total Employees: 1470
- Attrition Count: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16.1%
- Active Employees: 1233
- Average Monthly Income: ₹6.5K

### Dashboard Insights
- Highest attrition observed in R&D department
- Employees aged 25–34 show maximum attrition
- Life Sciences background has the highest attrition
- Sales Executives show varying job satisfaction levels
